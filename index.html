Our Mission

At The Normandy, we believe fine dining is an experience everyone deserves to enjoy. With authentically crafted menu dishes, expertly curated wines, and a soft-lit intimate atmosphere, we offer an experience second to none in the Lincoln restaurant scene. Come join us for an unforgettable taste of premiere French cuisine.
